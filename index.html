Satellites Story

Since 1957, the first satellite was launched into space. Hundreds of satellites are launched into Earth’s orbit every year, until April 2020, there are now 2,666 satellites. Over the coming decade, it’s estimated by Euroconsult that 990 satellites will be launched every year, which means that by 2028, there could be 15,000 satellites in orbit. Human has great interest in exploring the space. In the past, satellites were just used to navigation, while at present, satellites are becoming critical for everything from internet connectivity and precision agriculture, to border security and archaeological study. Satellites Application

Satellites are launched into space for a number of reasons. They do everything from military reconnaissance to keeping our GPS systems working properly. The truly global scope of telecommunications own to network of orbiting satellites. In general, Satellites are mainly classified by four application areas: Government, Military, Commercial and Civil.
